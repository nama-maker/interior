Interior design is the art & science of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve a healthier & more aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using the space. With a keen eye for detail & a creative flair,an interior designer is someone who plans, researches, coordinates, & manages such enhancement projects. Interior design is a multifaceted profession that includes space planning, programming, research, communicating with the stakeholders of a project,construction management & execution of the design. The profession of interior design has been a consequence of the development of society and the complex architecture that has resulted from the development of industrial processes. The pursuit of effective use of space, user well-being and functional design has contributed to the development of the contemporary interior design profession. The profession of interior design is separate and distinct from the role of interior decorator, a term commonly used in the US; the term is less common in the UK, where the profession of interior design is still unregulated and therefore, strictly speaking, not yet officially a profession.

Manit Rastogi

Founder-partner at Delhi-based company, Morphogenesis, Manit Rastogi is an expert at combining sound design with sustainability and creativity. His work has won several Indian and international design awards included being ranked as India’s top interior designer by several media houses.Some of his well-known projects include the Pearl Academy in Jaipur, Chettinad Health City Auditorium and Harley Davidson Corporate Office, Gurgaon.

Gauri Khan

The world knows Gauri Khan as the wife of Bollywood superstar Shahrukh Khan. However, most people are unaware that Gauri Khan owns her own firm, Gauri Khan Designs. She has worked on the homes of many Bollywood stars, making her one of the top celebrity interior designers in India. Gauri Khan is an interior designer who draws inspiration from her passion. This is what makes her designs truly unique, full of life, and outstanding. Her intense and impassioned designs have made Gauri Khan into a brand in her own right in the interior design industry.
